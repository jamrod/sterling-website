At Sterling our vision is to partner with healthcare and educational institutions by providing them with high quality integrated communication solutions from initial design through post-installation support. We do this by supplying superior design, products, installation and customer service at competitive prices.

  • Assisted living
  • Nursing Homes
  • Senior Living Communities
  • Schools
  • Corporate and Industrial Facilities
  • Military and Government Facilities
  • Correctional Facilities
