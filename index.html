About Us:

Sterling Communication Technologies, inc. is a Colorado based corporation established in 1994. We are a system integrator for internal communications in Healthcare, Senior Living and Educational Facilities as well as Special Applications. We focus on design, install and servicing of low voltage systems throughout Colorado, Wyoming, New Mexico as well as the Greater New Orleans area and Gulf Coast Region of Louisiana and Mississippi. Sterling provides exceptional 24/7 customer support.

Our Vision:

To partner with Healthcare, Educational and Senior Living Communities by providing the highest quality integrated solutions from conceptual design through implementation.

The very definition of the word "Sterling" means "of excellent quality, peerless, the best." This is the impression we strive for in everything we do.
