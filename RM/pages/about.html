About Us

Sterling Communication Technologies, Inc. was established in 1994. With corporate headquarters in Littleton, Colorado and a regional office in the Gulf Coast, Sterling provides sales, installation and support for low voltage communication systems in Colorado, New Mexico, Wyoming, and the Gulf Coast region of Louisiana and Mississippi.

Our Vision

To partner with healthcare and educational institutions by providing high quality integrated solutions from the design phase through implementation with exceptional 24/7 customer support.
