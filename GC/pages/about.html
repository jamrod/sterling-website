About Us

Sterling Communication Technologies, Inc. was established in 1994. In our Gulf Coast Regional office Sterling provides sales, installation and support for low voltage communication systems in Louisiana and Mississippi.

Our Vision

To partner with healthcare and educational institutions by providing high quality integrated solutions from the design phase through implementation with exceptional 24/7 customer support.
