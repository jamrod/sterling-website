About Us

Sterling Communication Technologies, Inc. was established in 1994. In our Gulf Coast Regional office Sterling provides sales, installation and support for low voltage communication systems in Louisiana and Mississippi.

With more than 100 years of combined expertise in the communication system industry, our certified sales staff has first-hand knowledge of our products as a result of many years of installation, service, design and development. This experience enables us to provide solutions to meet customer requirements.

Our customer service and installation staff is highly qualified to handle all types of service requests. Sterling's technicians are manufacturer certified and will accommodate your scheduling needs. Engineering is provided from design development through as-built drawings.

Our mission is to exceed customer expectations utilizing the highest quality systems, products, and services.
